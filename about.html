CoffeeBara Gives Back

Welcome to CoffeeBara Cafe, a non-profit coffeeshop that is committed to making a difference in the world by providing clean drinking water to developing countries around the world.

Our mission is simple:


To provide access to clean drinking water to those in need. We believe that every person has the right to this basic human necessity, and we are dedicated to making it a reality. Through our partnership with organizations that specialize in providing clean drinking water to developing countries, we are able to use a portion of every sale to support these efforts. With every cup of coffee or pastry that you purchase, you are directly contributing to our mission and making a positive impact on the lives of countless people. We believe in sustainability and ethical business practices, which is why we source our coffee from fair-trade and sustainable sources. This allows us to support coffee farmers and their families while also promoting sustainable farming practices that protect the environment.

At CoffeeBara Cafe, we are more than just a coffeeshop - we are a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for making the world a better place. We believe that through small actions and collective efforts, we can make a significant impact and create positive change in the world. Thank you for joining us on this journey to provide clean drinking water to developing countries around the world. Your support and patronage means the world to us, and we look forward to continuing to work together to make a difference.
